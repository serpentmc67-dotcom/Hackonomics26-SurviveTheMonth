⚙️ Core Mechanics
💰
Earn Money
Use the laptop to complete work tasks for bonus cash. Payday arrives weekly.
🍕
Eat Food
Your food level drops daily. Shop for groceries and eat from the fridge (3×/day).
🧠
Manage Sanity
Sanity decays from stress. Sleep, game, or visit the park to restore it.
📋
Pay Bills
Check the mail table each week for your salary + bills statement. Don't ignore it!
🧹
Clean Up
Dirt builds daily and caps your max sanity. Do chores to keep it under control.
📱
Handle Calls
Random phone calls arrive. Your choices have real consequences — think carefully!
💡 Learn As You Play — Every panel has a small ⓘ button. Tap it to learn a real financial concept — budgeting, bills, net income, and more.
⚠️ Game Over if:
Food hits 0%  •  Sanity hits 0%  •  Debt exceeds $500  •  Quit after Week 1
